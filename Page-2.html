About 

 

Welcome to my About page! My name is Amir, and I attended Penn State for college, where I studied Economics.

During my time at Penn State, I developed a passion for coding and programming. I have gained proficiency in several programming languages, including CSS, Python, STATA, and R, and I have experience working with Tableau, a data visualization tool. These skills have allowed me to analyze and interpret complex data sets, build mathematical models, and present my findings in an easy-to-understand format.

In pursuit of my career goals, I had the opportunity to intern at CBRE in the Financial Consulting Group, where I gained hands-on experience analyzing financial data using Argus Enterprise. Through this experience, I learned the importance of teamwork, communication, and attention to detail in a professional setting.

As a driven and motivated individual, I am committed to using my skills and knowledge to make a meaningful impact in the field of economics and beyond. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow, and I am excited to see where my journey will take me in the future.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
